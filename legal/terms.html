Authorised use

This app is for authorised research collectors only. You must follow study protocols, obtain verbal caregiver consent before collecting participant data, and enter accurate information.

Your responsibilities

  • Keep your login credentials confidential.
  • Enable location services while using the app as required for geo-tagging.

  • Report security incidents to your study coordinator promptly.

Payments

Collector payments are calculated from verified submissions according to rates set by administrators. Payment details (UPI) are used solely for disbursement.

You may delete your account from the Profile screen. Administrators may suspend accounts that violate study rules.
